The best movies according to Andrew

My top three movies of all time.

Toy Story

The first animation film I ever saw as a youngling. I was a year old when the film came out, and I think I saw it when I was around four or five. A classic Pixar film.

The Avengers

A fantastic superhero film. I saw it in theatres with some friends. I was a junior in high school.

Her

An unorthodox love story. I adore the awkwardness, and how the plot turns out. The plot goes in some different directions a traditional love plot goes and undermines the typical tropes.
